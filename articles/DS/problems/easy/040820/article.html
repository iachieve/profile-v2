Given a year, return the century it is in. The first century spans from the year 1 up to and including the year 100, the second - from the year 101 up to and including the year 200, etc.

Example For year = 1905, the output should be centuryFromYear(year) = 20; For year = 1700, the output should be centuryFromYear(year) = 17.

Solution 1: Century from a year

Math.ceil The Math.ceil function always rounds a number up to the next largest whole integer

Solution 2: Century from a year

Math.floor The Math.floor() function returns the largest integer less than or equal to a given number.
