About Me

I have a Bachelor's degree in Accounting with over six years of experience as a Software Engineer. In the early stages of my career in the accounting field, I worked on ERP systems and used excel sheets extensively.
At the time, the business required complex, customized features and reports that were not available. I took the initiative and was self-taught on how to program in C sharp and SQL to solve and streamline processes.
Ever since writing my first programming code to manipulate the output data, I have been committed to the idea of using software to solve practical everyday problems
I'm a software engineer who can prioritize, triage, and manage all of the various milestones of the multiple projects on my plate at any given time.
Software engineering is a never-ending puzzle that I am passionately engaged in solving. I believe in the power of programming to transform, improve, and streamline the process in the lives of people around the world.
